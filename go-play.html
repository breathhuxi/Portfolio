玩儿去 (Go and Play)


A platform to find activities, groups, skill exchange



Overview


This is a side project with some friends during COVID-19. It comes out from a normal catchup conversation. We found too many young Chinese people are losing fun in life to overtime work, and the whole society only urges consumerism on everyone: even options to a more diversified lifestyle are limited. The newly emerging mobile services in China emphasize too much on providing convenience, seizing attention, selling products (including "knowledge products"), etc. Meanwhile, a lot of people are suffering from anxiety, fatigue, depression, losing life purposes. We miss the good old days in school when there are many student associations, and people gather purely based on common interests or hobbies, to communicate face to face and physically spend time together.


We name this app "玩儿去", which literally sounds like a colloquial encouragement to go out and play. That is what we truly wish for our generation. We hope there can be a friendly community of grownups, giving each other company and support, to continue self discovery and have fun in the process.

Role


Product designer
User research, problem definition, competitive analysis, product positioning, persona and storyboard, prototyping, cognitive walkthrough, information architecture, wireframing, interaction, color scheme, high-fidelity mockups
